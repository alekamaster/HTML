About the Event

Our city proudly hosts an annual holiday race to raise awareness and funds for a local charity of our choosing. Families, friends, and athletes come together to celebrate health, joy, and generosity.

Whether you're running, cheering, or simply enjoying the festivities, your participation helps support our community and spread the holiday spirit.
