Current proyect

Esen is currently embarking on an ambitious journey: traveling across all of Mexico in just 80 days. His adventure has taken him to iconic places like Mineral del Chico, where he tried traditional dishes such as enchiladas mineras, pastes, and “tachuela,” while also exploring natural spots like the Vía Ferrata and Peña del Cuervo. He also visited the Mezquital Valley and Ixmiquilpan, enjoying barbacoa and tacos de Ximbo, before heading to the breathtaking Grutas de Tolantongo.


  • TikTok Awards 2024: In January 2024, Esen Alva was recognized as
    “Creator of the Year” at the TikTok Awards, cementing his place as one
    of Mexico’s top digital stars.
  • Panam Collaboration: In 2024, he launched a special sneaker collaboration with the iconic Mexican brand Panam.
    The design was deeply personal, inspired by his upbringing in the State of Mexico, with details like laces resembling laundry
    lines and shoebox art based on his childhood home
  • Podcast “Clase Libre” (2023): Alongside Islas Vlogs, Esen co-created the podcast Clase Libre on YouTube,
    where they shared funny school stories and invited major creators like Juanpa Zurita and El Javetas.
    The channel quickly grew past 700k subscribers.
  • Television “La Guasa”: In 2022, Esen was invited to co-host La Guasa on Distrito Comedia, proving his
    humor also resonates on traditional media.
