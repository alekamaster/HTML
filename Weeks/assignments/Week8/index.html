Welcome to New London Homes & Realty

We are a locally owned real estate agency proudly serving New London, Wisconsin and the surrounding towns. Our focus is single-family homes — from starter houses to lakeside retreats. You won't find commercial listings here; just honest, neighborhood-focused service.

Whether you are buying your first home, upsizing for a growing family, or looking to downsize, our agents bring local knowledge, practical advice, and the kind of old-fashioned courtesy that makes the closing day feel like a small celebration rather than a paperwork nightmare.
